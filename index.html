User Story

    As a layman who does not understand technology; he would like to make one click option on finding a pet.

    Usecase and Interaction Flow
      ----Title: Adopting a Pet----

  • Alex finds pet to adopt
  • Precondition: Alex has pet finder account, and he is sighed in.
  • Postcondtext-decoration: Alex files pet adoption documents.
  • Alex waits for response for pet agency to approve application.
  • Alex will chose a pet.
  • Alex will set an appointment with adoption facility.
  • Alex will interact with pet to see if they are a fit with each other.
  • Alex will make decision on what pet he will like to adopt.
  • Alex will pay fee to adopt pet.
  • Alex will take pet home.
